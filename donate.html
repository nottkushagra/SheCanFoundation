Dear Donor,

Every month, a 13-year-old girl in a small village is forced to miss five days of school. Not because she doesn't want to learn. Not because she is lazy. But because she cannot afford something as basic as a sanitary pad.

She hides at home in shame. She uses rags, newspapers, even sand — risking her health and her dignity. Slowly, her dreams of education, freedom, and self-respect begin to fade.

The Hard Truth 💔

  • ⚠️ 1 in every 5 girls in India drops out of school because of periods.
  • ⚠️ Over 12 crore women and girls in India still lack access to proper menstrual hygiene.
  • ⚠️ The result? Shame. Lost education. Lost opportunities.

But, dear donor — you hold the power to change this.

How You Can Save Her Dignity

At She Can Foundation, we have already helped 1,20,000+ girls across India with free sanitary pads, awareness workshops, and dignity kits. But for every 1 girl we reach, 5 more are still waiting. Right now, she waits… for you.

The next time you spend ₹300 on a coffee, remember: That same amount can provide 3 girls with a sanitary pad kit for an entire month. Your gift today is not charity. It is the difference between a girl living in shame or living with dignity.
