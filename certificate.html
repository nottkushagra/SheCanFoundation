She Can Foundation is registered under the Indian Society Registration Act XXI of 1860. As a registered NGO, we strictly comply with governmental standards, reporting regulations, and audits.

We believe in absolute transparency with our donors and volunteers. Every rupee contributed and every pad distributed is audited to ensure it directly supports menstrual hygiene and tech literacy programs across under-resourced schools and communities.
